CueRide Privacy Policy

1. What We Collect

We use your data to connect riders and drivers, process transactions, improve safety, and ensure compliance with legal obligations.

We do not sell your data. We share only with verified service providers (Stripe, Firebase) or authorities when required by law.

You may request, update, or delete your data anytime by contacting privacy@cuerideapp.com. CCPA/GDPR rights apply where applicable.

5. Security

We use encryption, role-based access, and cloud security protocols to protect user data.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. Parents may request deletion of minor data.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after changes implies acceptance.
