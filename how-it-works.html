How CueRide Works

Getting a sober-certified ride is simple. Here’s how it works:

  1. Open the app and request a ride like any other platform.
  2. Choose optional “CueMe Home” to get both you AND your car home.
  3. Use a sponsor code if someone prepaid your ride.
  4. Track your ride, rate your driver, and log your safe trip.
