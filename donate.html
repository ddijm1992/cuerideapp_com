Safe. Sober. On Cue.

CueRide is the first rideshare built for cue sports players, tournaments, and sober living support. Whether you're leaving league night, a late-night match, or just need a reliable sober ride — CueRide has you covered.

  • 🛡 Safe rides for APA, BCA, TAP and local leagues
  • ❤️ Powered by drivers in recovery communities
  • 🎯 Designed for tournament schedules & late hours
  • 📍 Book ahead for regionals, nationals, or local play
