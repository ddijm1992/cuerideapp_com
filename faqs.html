Frequently Asked Questions

Is CueRide like Uber or Lyft?

We offer sober-only rides with certified drivers, recovery integration, and car return options – not just convenience, but safety.

Do I need to be sober to drive?

Yes. All drivers undergo breathalyzer checks and training, in partnership with MADD and other public safety organizations.

Can I sponsor a ride for someone?

Yes! You can sponsor rides through our system and receive confirmation when they’re used safely.
